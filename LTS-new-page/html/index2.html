about us

Discover Our Story

Your premier provider in logistics, we have evolved into a dynamic leader in freight brokerage. Committed to excellence, we stand ready to deliver unparalleled service and efficiency for all your transportation needs.

At LTS, our mission is clear: deliver trust every mile of the way. Specializing in full truckload general freight, we act as the crucial link between shippers and carriers, leveraging our extensive network to secure optimal solutions at competitive prices. We provide coverage in the continental United States, Canada, and Mexico across all modes of transportation; Dry Van, Flatbed, Temp Controlled, Expedite, and Oversize.

Customer Centric Focus

Our customer-centric focus guides everything we do. We are dedicated to understanding our clients' needs, providing personalized solutions, and delivering exceptional service that exceeds their expectations.

Continuous Learning and Improvement

We are committed to continuous learning and improvement. Through ongoing education, innovation, and refinement of our processes, we strive to stay ahead of industry trends and deliver superior value to our clients.

Results Oriented

We are results-oriented and driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence. Our focus is on delivering measurable results and tangible outcomes that drive growth and success for our clients and partners.
